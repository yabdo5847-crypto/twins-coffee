Frequently Asked Questions

We use 100% premium Arabica beans, carefully hand-roasted in small batches to ensure an authentic and rich Turkish coffee experience.

We recommend storing your coffee in an airtight container in a cool, dry, and dark place. Avoid the fridge or freezer, as moisture can ruin the flavor.

Plain is our pure, rich, classic Turkish coffee. The Cardamom roast is infused with high-quality cardamom pods for a traditional, aromatic, and spiced flavor.

Orders are freshly packed and typically arrive within 2-4 business days depending on your exact location in Egypt.

We sell premium coffee blends as well as exclusive merchandise and coffee preparation gear to give you the ultimate brewing experience at home.
